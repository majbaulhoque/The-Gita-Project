SHARING WISHDOM

BY EXAMPLE

Our lives are shaped by stories - ones we tell ourselves, ones we hear - some we learn and some we feel. The impact of a story can be carried beyond our imagination, which is why we share the story of The Gita. Its pages offer an opportunity to be curious, to explore, gain deeper insights and discover new meanings. The Gita Project is a charity seeking to make the Gita accessible for all, wherever they are.

Words that inspire and stories that guide

MAKING SPACE TO LEARN FROM ancient wisdom

We create opportunities for exploration, connection and meaning. We do this through workshops, and by providing books and resources to organisations - any place someone is searching for inspiration. We also support chaplaincies and those offering pastoral care.

Together with our supporters, The Gita Project has moved from local beginnings to a truly global vision.
